About Us

Gbenda Intercessory Ministry

Gbenda Intercessory Ministry is a non-profit faith-based organization that is committed to helping individuals who want to develop and sustain an intimate relationship with God.


We are an online Christian Community created in January, 2023. Our mission is to share helpful tips.


Jesus AS The Way, The Truth and The Life is the full inspiration behind the Mobile Application and Website.


Our name “Gbenda” is from the Tiv language, of West Africa, meaning “ THE Way. ”
